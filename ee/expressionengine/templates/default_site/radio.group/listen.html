Listen with a Smartphone App

You can install an app your phone or tablet which will alow you to listen to the SHOUTcast feed.

We have tested the following apps:

  • StreamFurious (Android)
  • SHOUTcast Radio (iOS/iTunes)

Once you have installed the app, follow this link:
http://mp3.logosradionetwork.com/listen.pls.
Your installed app should automatically start playing the audio stream. If you're having problems, please contact us.

For more information and options, please visit the SHOUTcast website.
